# Week 7: Lesson 7.1  
## Applying Constraints, Parametric Diagrams and Instances with Simulation

The lesson for this week is to learn how to apply constraints among the parameters of your system, design parametric diagrams, and create instance tables that verify your constraints.

You will also learn how to run simulations from instance tables.

The example given here is a simple one, but it takes you through the steps from creating constraint blocks to Simulink and back to SysML. It shows the variation of diameter and height of a cylinder to calculate its volume.

The assignment attached requires that you demonstrate the application of these concepts in the **WDS project**.
